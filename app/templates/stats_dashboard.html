xwOBA vs wOBA (Luck Chart)

xwOBA: Expected performance based on contact quality. wOBA: Actual results.
Above the diagonal = overperforming (lucky). Below = underperforming (unlucky). Best way to find trade regression candidates.
Good: xwOBA .370+ Avg: .310-.369 Bad: < .310
Look for: Players below the diagonal — they're underperforming their expected stats and are buy-low targets. Players above are sell-high candidates.

{# Card: K% vs BB% #}

K% vs BB% (Plate Discipline)

K%: Strikeout rate — K = -0.5 pts. Lower is better. BB%: Walk rate — BB = +1 pt. Higher is better.
Discipline is worth ~1.5 pts per plate appearance event in your league.
Good: K% < 15%, BB% > 12% Avg: K% 15-22%, BB% 8-11% Bad: K% > 22%, BB% < 7%
Look for: Players in the top-left corner (low K% + high BB%) have the best discipline and earn the most free fantasy points.
