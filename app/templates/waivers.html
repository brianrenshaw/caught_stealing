Closers & Setup Men

A save is worth 7 points and a hold is worth 4. A closer converting 3 saves in a week generates 37+ points from one roster spot. A setup man with 2 holds quietly produces 20+. Look for the CL and SU role badges and the Fit score. When a closer hits the IL, the CLOSER OPP badge flags the next man up.

Contact Hitters (Low K%)

Every strikeout costs -0.5 points. Over a season, the gap between a 15% K hitter and a 28% K hitter is roughly 35 points — equivalent to 9 extra home runs. High plate discipline and low K rates are systematically undervalued. The BREAKOUT badge flags hitters whose Statcast metrics (barrel%, hard-hit%) are surging.

Two-Start Pitchers

Each inning pitched generates 4.5 base points from outs alone. A starter with two starts in one week can produce 40–60+ points — often more than any single hitter. The 2-START badge shows confirmed two-start pitchers with their opponents listed. This is the single highest-value weekly waiver strategy.

Schedule & Injuries

In weekly mode, the Games column shows how many games each team plays — hitters on 7-game teams get ~30% more PA than 5-game teams. DTD players have their weekly projection cut 50%. IL players are excluded entirely. Injury data comes from the MLB Official Injury Report.
